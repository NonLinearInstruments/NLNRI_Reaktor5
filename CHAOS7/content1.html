Rössler equations in Reaktor 5:

Rossler Midi.zip

Equal tempered, nine voice Rossler attractor instrument.
Midi notes trigger 9 copies of Rossler attractor, with C controlled by a fader, and inital value differences between voices controled by "init" pot.
Midi note number controlls integration step for the attractor with an equal tempered note number/integration factor relation.
The higher the C and "init" values, the more unstable becomes the instrument for higher MIDI notes (faster integration factor).
Clock division selector does not work right, due to an adjustment needed in core rossler cells. To be corrected in further versions.
